The world's Billionaires

Based on the Forbes' Billionaire List of 2021, this dashboard explores some statistics abouut the world's wealthiest people

Which countries have the most Billionaires?

The spread of the world's Billionaires spans all across the globe. This visualisation looks at the spread and concentration of Billionaires across the different countries and continents. Countries with a higher number of Billionaires are represented by a greater concentration of green. Currently the top 2 countries are China and the United States. North America currently has the most Billionaires.

A further analysis on the Billionaires of each country

The next bubble chart showcases the average age of each country's Billionaires and their average net worth. There is a slider filter which you can set a minimum net worth to filter out countries. France has the highest average net worth of Billionaires. The average age for Billionaires are in the older generation with Eswati (Swaziland) having the oldest average age.
